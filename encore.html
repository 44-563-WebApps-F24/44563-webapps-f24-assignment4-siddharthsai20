siddharth sai presents a greek play by sophocles

name of the play: king oedipus

Author: Sophocles

Oedipus

Do not speak in riddles, Tiresias! Tell me clearly what you know!

Tiresias

Sometimes, the truth is more painful than the silence you seek to avoid.

Oedipus

Then let me bear that pain! I will not turn away from what I must face!
